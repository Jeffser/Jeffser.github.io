Pigment

Get color palettes from images

Repository
Color Palettes Discover the colors hidden within your favorite images. Pigment lets you effortlessly extract beautiful and accurate color palettes from any image, all without needing an internet connection.

Whether you're designing a theme, creating artwork, or simply exploring color combinations, Pigment can generate up to 50 unique colors from a single image, helping you capture the perfect tone and mood every time.
Privacy
  • Offline: Pigment does not connect to the internet at all.
  • Open Source: Pigment is licensed under the GPL-3.0 license, it's code can be audited freely.
Colorthief The library Colorthief is used to do the heavy lifting, Pigment is basically a wrapper that gives users access to this tool in a nice UI with additional features.
