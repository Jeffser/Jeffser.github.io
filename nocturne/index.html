Nocturne

Bring your music library together

Repository
Music Library Organize your own music library with the features of streaming services and the speed and quality of local files.

Nocturne uses Navidrome to organize your library in different sections like albums, artists and even playlists.
Arrange Your Queue You have the flexibility to create your music queue so you can prepare all the music you want in the order you want.

Nocturne offers Autoplay as a way to always have somthing to listen to even if you don't feel like choosing music manually.
Automatic Lyrics Nocturne can download lyrics for your music automatically including synced lyrics that move with the song.

Perfect for karaoke sessions or for when you don't know exactly what the artist is singing in a certain song.
Performant Interface It doesn't matter if you have a couple of tracks or hundreds, you can navigate through every song, album, artist and playlist efficiently.

Nocturne keeps cache information of most elements in your library, reducing load times and making the experience as smooth as possible.
Fully Featured Nocturne can group songs by their album, aritst and playlists, making it easier to find something to play.

The interface was made from the ground up to be easy on the eyes but still powerful and efficient.
Privacy
  • Telemetry: Nocturne does not have any type of telemetry whatsoever.
  • Library: Your music belongs to you, Nocturne won't collect any information on it.
  • Open Source: Nocturne is licensed under the GPL-3.0 license, it's code can be audited freely.
Supporting Artists Nocturne does not facilitate nor supports the piracy of content, any music shown in screenshots has been purchased or have been made freely available by the original artists. (bangers btw)

You can obtain music legally by using sites such as Bandcamp or Patreon. Remember, any ammount of money given directly to artists supports their work and the music you enjoy.
