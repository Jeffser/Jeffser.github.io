Documentation • Repository
Chat With AI Models Enjoy seamless interactions with advanced AI models directly on your device—no data collection, no cloud dependencies, and complete control over your privacy. Ask deep, meaningful questions on complex topics or leverage the power of AI to analyze files like PDFs or even images to get insightful answers.

Getting started is simple and hassle-free. Just install Alpaca, choose and download the AI model that suits your needs, and start exploring the possibilities—all without the need for complex setups or technical expertise.
Image Recognition Ask image recognition-capable models insightful questions about any image, all while keeping your data secure and offline. Your images and text stay completely private, ensuring everything remains between you and the model.
Custom Models Create personalized AI models by simply providing instructions and a profile picture. Perfect for crafting immersive roleplay scenarios with your favorite fictional characters or even developing unique AI personas tailored to your needs.

Unleash your creativity and make every interaction feel truly personal. Whether it's for entertainment, storytelling, or brainstorming, Alpaca lets you design models that fit your vision seamlessly.
Powered by Local AI Dive into a vast collection of pre-trained models from leading innovators like Meta, Microsoft, Google, Nvidia, and Alibaba, as well as unique independent models, all accessible through a user-friendly interface.

From coding assistants and multilingual communicators to advanced image recognition and specialized expertise, Alpaca gives you the power to experiment with a wide range of capabilities. Even better, you can combine multiple models in a single conversation, ensuring you always have the perfect tool to answer any question or tackle any challenge.
Privacy The model list used by Alpaca is provided by Ollama, ensuring secure, offline operation. Models have no direct access to the internet, and all information is stored locally on your device.
  • Local Data Storage: All conversations are securely saved in SQLite3 files on your device.
  • Offline Capability: Once your models are downloaded, you can use Alpaca entirely offline.
Open Source Alpaca is fully open source and licensed under the GPL-3.0 license, ensuring complete transparency and trustworthiness. Every line of its code is available for inspection, allowing anyone to verify its integrity, contribute to its development, or adapt it to their needs.

By embracing open-source principles, Alpaca empowers its users with full control and confidence in their AI experience.
